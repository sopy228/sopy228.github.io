About London

       London is a city in Ontario, Canada with a population of 383, 822 (as of 2016).

School and Education

One of the main elemantary and secondary public school board is the Thames Valley District School Board. They are refered to as TVDSB and govern London and surrounding area. There is also a London Catholic School Board and 2 main private schools; Matthews Hall and the Montissori Academy.

Shopping

London has 2 main malls, Masonville Place and Whiteoaks mall. Masonville is owned by the company Cadillac Fairview which owns many malls across Ontario. Whiteoaks mall is London based and does not belong to a retail chain.

Thing to do

There are many things you can do while in London. At Victoria park, you can enjoy frequent festivals or shop around at the street shops nearby. London also has a pioneer village, great for kids and learning about the early settlers. You could drop by the Covent Garden Market for lunch and then walk across the street to Budweiser Gardens to watch a hockey game or a thrilling performance of some kind.
